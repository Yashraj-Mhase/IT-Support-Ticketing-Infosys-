Week 1 – Project Understanding & Power BI Fundamentals

In the first week, we were introduced to the project domain based on customer transaction issues handled through ticketing systems.
We learned the fundamentals of Power BI, including Ribbon, Power Query, Report View, Table View, and Model View. We also explored multiple data sources and practiced data extraction from online platforms.
Using Power Query, we performed essential data cleaning tasks such as removing duplicates, handling missing values, eliminating unnecessary columns, standardizing data, trimming whitespaces, replacing values, and splitting columns using delimiters.
